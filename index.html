ABOUT US

Bringing quality maids to Malaysian homes.

We at Harmoni are a maid and foreign workers agency with over 10 years of experience in the industry. We specialize in providing Indonesian and Filipino maids to Malaysians by partnering with reliable and reputable recruitment agencies overseas. We run a legitimate business as we are one of few certified maid providers in Malaysia. We aim to ensure a smooth and pleasant experience during the process from start to end.
